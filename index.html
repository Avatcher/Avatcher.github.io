About me

I am a Ukrainian student, who enjoys coding, particurarly using Java, C++ and just a little bit of Rust. Besides programming I also have other hobbies:

  • Drawing:


    I am not really good in drawing, as I get frustrated everytime I fail to draw something, although the nische I partially mastered is Pixel-Art. I started to draw it as fan-arts for some videogames, and at some point I just could draw well enough, I could be proud of. I still have no idea how to draw people, but what I can is pretty much enough for my needs. I find drawing more userful skill, rather than something, that brings me happiness.

  • Videogames:


    I have played videogames since very young age and the game, that will forever remain in my heart, is of course Minecraft, that partially made me into programming in the first place thanks to so-called "command blocks".

    Although the game, that really inspired me to start programming was Terraria. Back then I was a part of one game's fan-community and decided to help one guy with creating a mod for the game by drawing sprites for him. But there was a problem: I always had too many ideas for the mod, that I used to tell him. Sadly he just could not do everything himself, even if he wanted, so I decided to start learning programming myself, partially to help him to develop the mod even more and to make my own ideas happen. So became C# my first programming language.

  • Books:


    Reading has always helped me to keep my head together. The best book, and the book, that made me into reading, surely was Dune of Herbert Frank. It is incredible story, that shared with me a ton of unusual and interesting ideas, that made me think a lot and changed how I look at the world around me. It is totally worth reading, although not everyone might like it, as I did.
